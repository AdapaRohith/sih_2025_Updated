MADHYA PRADESH

The Heart of India

Discover the rich cultural heritage, magnificent temples, and vibrant traditions of Madhya Pradesh. From the ancient temples of Khajuraho to the beautiful wildlife sanctuaries, explore the heart of India.

Key Facts

  • Capital: Bhopal
  • Population: 72 Million
  • Best Time: Oct-Mar
  • Language: Hindi

Introduction

Madhya Pradesh is a state rich in history, culture, and natural beauty. Known as the "Heart of India," Madhya Pradesh is famous for its magnificent temples, ancient monuments, and diverse wildlife. From the world-famous Khajuraho temples to the beautiful national parks, Madhya Pradesh offers a perfect blend of architectural marvels, cultural heritage, and natural wonders.

Khajuraho Group of Monuments

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, these temples are famous for their intricate sculptures and architectural beauty.

Sanchi Stupa

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, this Buddhist monument is one of the oldest stone structures in India.

Gwalior Fort

A hill fort near Gwalior, known for its impressive architecture and historical significance.

Dhuandhar Falls

A spectacular waterfall on the Narmada River near Jabalpur, known for its marble rocks and misty spray creating a smoke-like effect.
