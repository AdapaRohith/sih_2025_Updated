HIMACHAL PRADESH

The Land of Snow

Discover the rich cultural heritage, magnificent temples, and vibrant traditions of Himachal Pradesh. From the beautiful hill stations to the ancient temples, explore the land of snow.

Key Facts

  • Capital: Shimla
  • Population: 6.8 Million
  • Best Time: Apr-Jun, Sep-Nov
  • Language: Hindi

Introduction

Himachal Pradesh is a state rich in history, culture, and natural beauty. Known as the "Land of Snow," Himachal Pradesh is famous for its beautiful hill stations, ancient temples, and scenic landscapes. From the popular hill station of Shimla to the spiritual town of Dharamshala, Himachal Pradesh offers a perfect blend of adventure, spirituality, and natural beauty.

Search Monuments & Places in Himachal Pradesh

Shimla

The capital city and a popular hill station offering panoramic views of the surrounding mountains, colonial architecture, and pleasant climate.

Manali

A popular hill station known for its scenic beauty, adventure sports, and pleasant climate.

Dharamshala & McLeod Ganj

The residence of the Dalai Lama and a center of Tibetan culture in India, known for its spiritual atmosphere and Buddhist monasteries.

Spiti Valley

A high-altitude desert valley known for its stark beauty, ancient monasteries, and unique Tibetan culture in the trans-Himalayan region.
