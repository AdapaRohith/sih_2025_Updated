Rajasthan

Discover the royal heritage, magnificent palaces, and vibrant culture of Rajasthan. From the golden sands of Jaisalmer to the pink city of Jaipur, explore the land of kings.

Search Monuments & Places in Rajasthan

Hawa Mahal

The Palace of Winds is a five-story palace in Jaipur, built with red and pink sandstone. It was designed to allow royal ladies to observe street festivals without being seen.

Amber Fort

Amber Fort is a magnificent fort located in Amer, Rajasthan. It's known for its artistic Hindu style elements and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

City Palace, Jaipur

The City Palace is a palace complex in Jaipur, which includes the Chandra Mahal and Mubarak Mahal palaces and other buildings.

Jantar Mantar

Jantar Mantar is a collection of nineteen architectural astronomical instruments built by the Rajput king Sawai Jai Singh II.

Udaipur City Palace

The City Palace in Udaipur is a palace complex built over a period of nearly 400 years, with contributions from several rulers of the Mewar dynasty.

Jaisalmer Fort

Jaisalmer Fort is one of the largest fully preserved fortified cities in the world, built in 1156 AD by the Rajput ruler Rawal Jaisal.
