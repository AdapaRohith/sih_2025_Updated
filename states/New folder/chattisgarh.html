CHHATTISGARH

The Rice Bowl of India

Discover the rich cultural heritage, magnificent temples, and vibrant traditions of Chhattisgarh. From the ancient temples of Sirpur to the beautiful waterfalls, explore the rice bowl of India.

Key Facts

  • Capital: Raipur
  • Population: 25 Million
  • Best Time: Oct-Mar
  • Language: Hindi

Introduction

Chhattisgarh is a state rich in history, culture, and natural beauty. Known as the "Rice Bowl of India," Chhattisgarh is famous for its fertile agricultural lands, magnificent temples, and rich tribal culture. From the ancient Sirpur temples to the beautiful waterfalls, Chhattisgarh offers a perfect blend of architectural heritage, natural beauty, and cultural diversity.
