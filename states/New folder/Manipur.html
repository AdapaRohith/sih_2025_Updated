MANIPUR

The Jewel of India

Discover the rich cultural heritage, magnificent temples, and vibrant traditions of Manipur. From the beautiful Kangla Fort to the unique Loktak Lake, explore the jewel of India.

Key Facts

  • Capital: Imphal
  • Population: 2.7 Million
  • Best Time: Oct-Mar
  • Language: Manipuri

Introduction

Manipur is a state rich in history, culture, and natural beauty. Known as the "Jewel of India," Manipur is famous for its unique culture, beautiful landscapes, and traditional dance forms. From the historic Kangla Fort to the unique Loktak Lake, Manipur offers a perfect blend of cultural heritage, natural beauty, and traditional arts.
