ANDHRA PRADESH

The Kohinoor of India

Discover the rich cultural heritage, ancient temples, and vibrant traditions of Andhra Pradesh. From the majestic Tirupati Temple to the beautiful beaches of Visakhapatnam, explore the heart of South India.

Key Facts

  • Capital: Amaravati
  • Population: 49 Million
  • Best Time: Oct-Mar
  • Language: Telugu

Introduction

Andhra Pradesh, often called the "Kohinoor of India," is a state rich in history, culture, and natural beauty. Known for its ancient temples, classical dance forms like Kuchipudi, and delicious cuisine, the state offers a perfect blend of tradition and modernity. From the sacred Tirupati Temple to the beautiful beaches of Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh is a treasure trove of cultural heritage.
