TRIPURA

The Land of Rich Heritage

Discover the rich cultural heritage, magnificent temples, and vibrant traditions of Tripura. From the beautiful Ujjayanta Palace to the unique Neermahal, explore the land of rich heritage.

Key Facts

  • Capital: Agartala
  • Population: 3.6 Million
  • Best Time: Oct-Mar
  • Language: Bengali, Kokborok

Introduction

Tripura is a state rich in history, culture, and natural beauty. Known as the "Land of Rich Heritage," Tripura is famous for its magnificent palaces, beautiful temples, and rich tribal culture. From the historic Ujjayanta Palace to the unique Neermahal water palace, Tripura offers a perfect blend of royal heritage, natural beauty, and cultural diversity.

Search Monuments & Places in Tripura

Ujjayanta Palace

A former royal palace now serving as a museum, showcasing the rich heritage of Tripura.

Neermahal

A beautiful water palace built in the middle of Rudrasagar Lake, unique architectural marvel.

Tripura Sundari Temple

A famous Hindu temple dedicated to Goddess Tripura Sundari, one of the 51 Shakti Peethas.

Unakoti

An ancient pilgrimage site with rock-cut sculptures, known as the 'Angkor Wat of Northeast India'.
