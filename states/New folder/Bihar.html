Discover the rich cultural heritage, ancient Buddhist sites, and vibrant traditions of Bihar. From the sacred Bodh Gaya to the ancient Nalanda University, explore the land of enlightenment.

Key Facts

  • Capital: Patna
  • Population: 104 Million
  • Best Time: Oct-Mar
  • Language: Hindi

Introduction

Bihar is a state rich in history, culture, and spiritual significance. Known as the "Land of Enlightenment," Bihar is home to some of the most important Buddhist and Hindu pilgrimage sites in India. From the sacred Bodh Gaya where Buddha attained enlightenment to the ancient Nalanda University, Bihar offers a perfect blend of spiritual heritage and historical significance.

Bodh Gaya

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, this is where Buddha attained enlightenment under the Bodhi tree.

Nalanda University

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, this ancient center of learning was one of the world's first universities.

Patna Sahib

One of the five Takhts of Sikhism, this gurudwara is dedicated to Guru Gobind Singh.

Vikramshila University

An ancient center of Buddhist learning, established by the Pala dynasty.
