ODISHA

The Soul of Incredible India

Discover the rich cultural heritage, magnificent temples, and vibrant traditions of Odisha. From the iconic Sun Temple of Konark to the beautiful beaches of Puri, explore the soul of India.

Key Facts

  • Capital: Bhubaneswar
  • Population: 41 Million
  • Best Time: Oct-Mar
  • Language: Odia

Introduction

Odisha is a state rich in history, culture, and natural beauty. Known as the "Soul of Incredible India," Odisha is famous for its magnificent temples, beautiful beaches, and rich cultural heritage. From the world-famous Konark Sun Temple to the sacred Jagannath Temple in Puri, Odisha offers a perfect blend of architectural marvels, spiritual significance, and natural beauty.

Search Monuments & Places in Odisha

Konark Sun Temple

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, this 13th-century temple is dedicated to the Sun God and is famous for its architectural grandeur.

Jagannath Temple

A famous Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Jagannath, located in Puri. It's one of the Char Dham pilgrimage sites.

Lingaraja Temple

A Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, located in Bhubaneswar. It's one of the oldest temples in Odisha.

Chilika Lake

A brackish water lagoon and the largest coastal lagoon in India, famous for its biodiversity and migratory birds.
