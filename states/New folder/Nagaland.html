NAGALAND

The Land of Festivals

Discover the rich cultural heritage, magnificent landscapes, and vibrant traditions of Nagaland. From the historic Kohima War Cemetery to the beautiful Dzukou Valley, explore the land of festivals.

Key Facts

  • Capital: Kohima
  • Population: 1.9 Million
  • Best Time: Oct-May
  • Language: English, Nagamese

Introduction

Nagaland is a state rich in history, culture, and natural beauty. Known as the "Land of Festivals," Nagaland is famous for its vibrant tribal culture, beautiful landscapes, and rich traditions. From the historic Kohima War Cemetery to the beautiful Dzukou Valley, Nagaland offers a perfect blend of natural beauty, cultural heritage, and traditional lifestyle.
