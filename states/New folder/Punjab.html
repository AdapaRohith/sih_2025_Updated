PUNJAB

The Land of Five Rivers

Discover the rich cultural heritage, magnificent gurudwaras, and vibrant traditions of Punjab. From the sacred Golden Temple to the beautiful countryside, explore the heart of northern India.

Key Facts

  • Capital: Chandigarh
  • Population: 27 Million
  • Best Time: Oct-Mar
  • Language: Punjabi

Introduction

Punjab is a state rich in history, culture, and natural beauty. Known as the "Land of Five Rivers," Punjab is famous for its magnificent gurudwaras, vibrant culture, and fertile agricultural lands. From the sacred Golden Temple to the beautiful countryside, Punjab offers a perfect blend of spiritual heritage, cultural diversity, and natural beauty.

Search Monuments & Places in Punjab

Golden Temple

The holiest shrine of Sikhism, also known as Harmandir Sahib. It's a symbol of human brotherhood and equality.

Jallianwala Bagh

A public garden in Amritsar, historically important as the site of the 1919 Jallianwala Bagh massacre.

Virasat-e-Khalsa

A world-class museum in Anandpur Sahib showcasing 500 years of Sikh history, culture, and heritage through interactive exhibits.

Qila Mubarak

A historic fort complex in Patiala, showcasing Punjabi architecture and the rich heritage of the Patiala princely state.
