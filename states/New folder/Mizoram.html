MIZORAM

The Land of the Hill People

Discover the rich cultural heritage, magnificent landscapes, and vibrant traditions of Mizoram. From the beautiful Phawngpui to the unique Solomon's Temple, explore the land of the hill people.

Key Facts

  • Capital: Aizawl
  • Population: 1.1 Million
  • Best Time: Oct-Mar
  • Language: Mizo

Introduction

Mizoram is a state rich in history, culture, and natural beauty. Known as the "Land of the Hill People," Mizoram is famous for its pristine landscapes, rich tribal culture, and beautiful hill stations. From the magnificent Phawngpui peak to the unique Solomon's Temple, Mizoram offers a perfect blend of natural beauty, cultural heritage, and traditional lifestyle.

Search Monuments & Places in Mizoram

Phawngpui (Blue Mountain)

The highest peak in Mizoram, also known as the Blue Mountain, offering breathtaking views.

Aizawl

The capital city of Mizoram, known for its scenic hilltop location, vibrant culture, and beautiful architecture including Solomon's Temple.

Vantawng Falls

One of the tallest waterfalls in Mizoram, cascading from a height of 751 feet, surrounded by lush green forests.

Dampa Tiger Reserve

A wildlife sanctuary with diverse flora and fauna, known for its conservation efforts.
