Introduction

Uttar Pradesh is a state rich in history, culture, and natural beauty. Known as the "Heart of India," Uttar Pradesh is home to some of the most iconic monuments and sacred sites in India. From the magnificent Taj Mahal to the sacred city of Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh offers a perfect blend of architectural marvels, spiritual heritage, and cultural diversity.

Search Monuments & Places in Uttar Pradesh

Taj Mahal

A UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the Seven Wonders of the World. A magnificent white marble mausoleum built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan.

Varanasi

The sacred city of Varanasi is famous for its ghats along the Ganges River, where pilgrims perform religious rituals.

Lucknow

The city of Nawabs, known for its rich cultural heritage, Mughal and British colonial architecture, and exquisite cuisine and etiquette.

Ayodhya

The birthplace of Lord Rama, this ancient city is one of the seven sacred cities in Hinduism and holds immense religious significance.
