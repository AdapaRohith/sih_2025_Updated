UTTARAKHAND

The Land of Gods

Discover the rich cultural heritage, magnificent temples, and vibrant traditions of Uttarakhand. From the sacred Char Dham to the beautiful hill stations, explore the land of gods.

Key Facts

  • Capital: Dehradun
  • Population: 10 Million
  • Best Time: Apr-Jun, Sep-Nov
  • Language: Hindi

Introduction

Uttarakhand is a state rich in history, culture, and natural beauty. Known as the "Land of Gods," Uttarakhand is famous for its sacred pilgrimage sites, beautiful hill stations, and stunning landscapes. From the sacred Char Dham to the beautiful hill stations of Mussoorie and Nainital, Uttarakhand offers a perfect blend of spiritual heritage, natural beauty, and adventure tourism.

Search Monuments & Places in Uttarakhand

Char Dham

Four sacred Hindu pilgrimage sites: Yamunotri, Gangotri, Kedarnath, and Badrinath.

Mussoorie

A popular hill station known as the 'Queen of Hills', offering beautiful views and pleasant climate.

Rishikesh

The 'Yoga Capital of the World', a sacred city on the banks of the Ganges, known for spiritual retreats, adventure sports, and ashrams.

Auli

A premier skiing destination in the Himalayas, offering breathtaking views of Nanda Devi and other peaks, perfect for winter sports.
