Key Facts

  • Best Time: Oct-Mar
  • Language: English, Hindi

Introduction

Arunachal Pradesh is a state rich in history, culture, and natural beauty. Known as the "Land of Dawn-lit Mountains," Arunachal Pradesh is famous for its pristine landscapes, diverse tribal culture, and magnificent monasteries. From the ancient Tawang Monastery to the beautiful Namdapha National Park, Arunachal Pradesh offers a perfect blend of spiritual heritage, natural beauty, and cultural diversity.

Search Monuments & Places in Arunachal Pradesh

Bomdila Monastery

A beautiful Buddhist monastery located in Bomdila, known for its peaceful atmosphere and stunning mountain views. An important center for Buddhist learning and meditation.

Malinithan Temple

An ancient archaeological site and temple complex dating back to the 14th-15th century. Known for its historical significance and beautiful stone carvings.

Ita Fort

An ancient fort built with mud and brick, known for its unique architecture and historical significance. A testament to the engineering skills of ancient Arunachal Pradesh.

Golden Pagoda

A magnificent golden Buddhist pagoda in Namsai district, known for its stunning architecture and spiritual significance. A symbol of peace and tranquility.
