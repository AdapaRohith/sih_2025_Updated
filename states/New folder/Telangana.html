TELANGANA

The New State

Discover the rich cultural heritage, magnificent monuments, and vibrant traditions of Telangana. From the historic Charminar to the beautiful Hussain Sagar Lake, explore the new state.

Key Facts

  • Capital: Hyderabad
  • Population: 35 Million
  • Best Time: Oct-Mar
  • Language: Telugu

Introduction

Telangana is a state rich in history, culture, and natural beauty. Known as the "New State," Telangana is famous for its magnificent monuments, vibrant culture, and technological advancement. From the historic Charminar to the beautiful Hussain Sagar Lake, Telangana offers a perfect blend of ancient heritage, modern development, and cultural diversity.

Search Monuments & Places in Telangana

Charminar

A historic monument and mosque in Hyderabad, built in 1591, symbol of the city.

Golconda Fort

A historic fort known for its acoustic architecture and diamond trade history.

Ramappa Temple

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, this 13th-century Kakatiya temple is renowned for its intricate sculptures and architectural brilliance.

Thousand Pillar Temple

A historic Kakatiya temple in Warangal, famous for its star-shaped architecture and intricate stone carvings from the 12th century.
